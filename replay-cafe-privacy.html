Privacy Policy

for Replay Cafe — Effective August 14, 2025

Thank you for playing Replay Cafe (“the Game”, “we”, “us”, or “our”). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not install or use the Game.

Summary

  • We designed Replay Cafe to collect as little personal data as possible.
  • We may process limited technical data for functionality, performance, and crash diagnostics.
  • We do not sell your data.
  • You can contact us anytime to ask questions or request deletion of data we control.

Information We Collect

1) Information you provide

  • Support inquiries: If you email us, we receive your email address and any information you include.
  • Game Center (optional): If you sign in to Apple Game Center, Apple may share a player identifier and achievements/leaderboard data with the Game as needed. You can manage this in iOS settings.

2) Information collected automatically

  • Device and usage data: Basic device information (e.g., device model, OS version, language), anonymized session metrics (e.g., session length, screens viewed), and performance data to understand how the Game is used and improve stability.
  • Crash diagnostics: Crash logs and error reports may be collected to fix bugs and improve performance.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate Replay Cafe.
  • Monitor performance, fix bugs, and improve gameplay balance.
  • Understand aggregate usage to inform new features and level design.
  • Respond to player support requests.

Legal Bases (EEA/UK users)

Where applicable, we process data on the basis of legitimate interests (e.g., app functionality, security, crash diagnostics) and/or your consent (e.g., analytics where required). You may withdraw consent at any time via device settings or by contacting us.

Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited information with service providers that help us operate the Game, such as:

  • Platform services: Apple (App Store, Game Center, iCloud backups where applicable).
  • Diagnostics: Apple’s built-in crash reporting; other crash/analytics providers only if configured.

These providers process data on our behalf and according to their own privacy terms and settings. Please review your device and platform privacy controls.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, after which we will delete or anonymize it unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Your Choices & Controls

  • Opt out of analytics: Use your device’s privacy settings to limit or reset advertising identifiers, analytics, or diagnostics.
  • Game Center: You can sign out of Game Center or manage what is shared.
  • Access/Deletion: Contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of data we control.

Children’s Privacy

Replay Cafe is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We take steps to ensure appropriate protections are in place when data is transferred.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the “Effective” date above and, where required, notify you via the App Store listing or in-game notice.

Contact Us

If you have questions or requests regarding privacy, please contact us at: privacy@replay.cafe
